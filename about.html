    This page tells you a little bit about me and my blog.

    I am Jack, a 19-year-old first-year Computer Science student. I am very interested in artificial intelligence, alignment, AI saftey, AI ethics, and neural networks.

    This blog is a place for me to organise my thoughts and my notes whilst I go through the process of trying to understand artificial intelligence and the impact that it is going to have on the world.

    This blog is primarly a resource for me, whereas the main arinteli site is primarily a resource for others. Therefore posts here may be less guided or flat out incomprehensible, whereas I will try my best to make main site posts welcoming and well explained. In general, I will try to keep my thoughts and opinions on this blog, whilst keeping informative posts on the main site.

    This blog will also hopefully serve as writing practice for me. I will try to improve my style and coherence with each post I publish.
